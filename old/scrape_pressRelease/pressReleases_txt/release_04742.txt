A federal court in Orlando, Florida, has permanently barred two managers of tax preparation stores in Florida from preparing tax returns for others and from owning or operating a tax return preparation business, the Justice Department announced today.The civil injunction order, to which Jehoakim Victor and Lauri Rodriguez agreed, was signed by Chief Judge Anne C. Conway of the U.S. District Court for the Middle District of Florida.NoneThe lawsuit against Pierre-Louis, Rodriguez and Victor is one of eight suits filed by the United States in Florida against Walner Gachette, the founder and franchisor of LBS Tax Services, and numerous other LBS franchisees and managers. The other cases, as well as the claims against Pierre-Louis in this case, remain pending.None
