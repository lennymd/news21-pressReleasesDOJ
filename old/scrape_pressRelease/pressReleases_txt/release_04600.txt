On Feb. 27, 2015, the Director of National Intelligence declassified and disclosed publicly that the U.S. government had filed an application with the Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Court seeking renewal of the authority to collect telephony metadata in bulk, and that the FISC renewed that authority.The FISC's Feb. 26, 2015, Primary Order renewing the collection expires on June 1, 2015. The DNI also announced that the Administration was undertaking a declassification review of the Feb. 26, 2015, Primary Order. Following this review by the Executive Branch, the ODNI has released in redacted form the Feb. 26, 2015, Primary Order, signed by Judge James E. Boasberg.NoneNone
