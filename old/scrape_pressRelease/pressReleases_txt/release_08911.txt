      WASHINGTON  A federal court in Mobile, Ala., has permanently barred Delois Warren from preparing federal tax returns for others, the Justice Department announced today.  Judge Kristi K. DuBose of U.S. District Court for the Southern District of Alabama issued the permanent injunction order. None       The civil injunction order requires Warren to mail a copy of the order to all persons for whom she has prepared a federal tax return since Jan. 1, 2007, and to give the government a list of those customers.
