A federal court has permanently barred Annie P. Williams, the former proprietor of PPH Tax & Realty Inc. in Brooklyn, N.Y., from preparing federal tax returns for others and from having any ownership or financial interest in any tax preparation business, the Justice Department announced today. The civil injunction order, to which Williams consented without admitting the allegations against her, was entered by Judge Dora L.
