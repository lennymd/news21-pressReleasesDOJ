A Portland, Oregon resident was sentenced to serve 24 months in prison for aggravated identity theft, announced Acting Deputy Assistant Attorney General Stuart M. Goldberg of the Justice Departments Tax Division and U.S. Attorney Billy Williams for the District of Oregon. None In addition to the term of prison imposed, Brown was ordered to serve one year of supervised release and to pay $2,000 in restitution to Central City Concern, to reimburse the organization for costs it incurred as a result of Browns identity theft.
