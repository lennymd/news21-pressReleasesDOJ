NoneThe indictment charges Robert Lee Foster, 65, of De Pere, Wisconsin, with nine counts of wire fraud.According to the indictment, from 2008 through May 2015, Foster devised a scheme to fraudulently obtain money and property from at least three women, whom Foster targeted because of their age, health, marital or family status, or other personal circumstances.
