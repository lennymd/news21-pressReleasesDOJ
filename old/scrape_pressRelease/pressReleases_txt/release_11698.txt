  WASHINGTON - A federal judge in Sioux City, Iowa, today barred a Humboldt, Iowa, woman, Gayle Lemmon, and her tax preparation business from claiming improper deductions on federal income tax returns. The courts order, which Lemmon agreed to, also prohibits her from representing customers before the IRS and requires her to attend government-approved tax training classes.None  According to the complaint, the IRS examined approximately 243 returns that Lemmon prepared and found that 224 of them understated the customers tax liabilities. The complaint alleges that the tax loss from Lemmons alleged misconduct between 2003 and 2008 could be as much as $17 million.None
