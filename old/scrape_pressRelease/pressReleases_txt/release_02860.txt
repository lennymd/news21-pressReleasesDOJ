Attorney General Loretta E. Lynch released the following statement Tuesday on the departure, effective April 14, of Acting Associate Attorney General Stuart F. Delery:For over seven years  since the very first day of the Obama Administration  Stuart Delery has been an indispensable source of wisdom, leadership and inspiration at the Department of Justice, working relentlessly to make the ideals of equal opportunity and equal justice a reality for all. He has proven himself a superlative lawyer and a dynamic force for progress, both within the department and far beyond it. Stuart was involved in many of the departments most consequential cases and programs, and he invariably brought both skill and passion to a wide range of critical issues.None
