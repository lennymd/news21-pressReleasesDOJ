NoneNoneNone   The U.S. Trade Representative (USTR) reviewed the exclusion order issued by the ITC against Apple at Samsungs request and overturned it, determining that it was not consistent with the public interest.   As a result of the USTRs action, the Antitrust Division has determined that no further action is required at this time.   The Antitrust Division is therefore closing its investigation into Samsungs conduct, but will continue to monitor further developments in this area.   Throughout the investigation, the Antitrust Division has worked closely and consulted frequently with its colleagues at the European Commission. This cooperation underscores the agencies common concerns over the potential harm to competition that can result from the anticompetitive use of SEPs.  None None
