  WASHINGTON  Attorney General Eric Holder recognizes 247 department employees for their distinguished public service today at the 57th Annual Attorney General Awards Ceremony. Thirty-nine other individuals outside of the department are also honored for their work. Held at DAR Constitution Hall, this annual ceremony recognizes both department employees and others for their dedication to carrying out the Department of Justices mission.  It is my privilege to honor these recipients for their outstanding service and commitment to our country, Attorney General Holder said. Each of these dedicated servants has carried out the important mission of the Department of Justice and has done so with excellence and distinction.
