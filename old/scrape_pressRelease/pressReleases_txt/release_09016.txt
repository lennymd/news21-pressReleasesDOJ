None     The department will also investigate allegations that SCI Cresson provided inadequate mental health care to prisoners who have mental illness, failed to adequately protect such prisoners from harm, and subjected them to excessively prolonged periods of isolation, in violation of the Eighth Amendment to the U.S. Constitution.     The Justice Department will seek to determine whether there is a pattern or practice of violations of the Constitution by officers or staff at SCI Cresson and SCI Pittsburgh.  During the course of the investigation, the Justice Department will consider all relevant information.  The investigation will include visits to the facilities, a review of records, interviews with corrections officials, prisoners and other witnesses, including family and community members, and advocates.  The Justice Department has taken similar steps involving a variety of state and local correctional facilities, both large and small.
