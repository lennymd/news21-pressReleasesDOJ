      WASHINGTON - A federal court in Beaumont, Texas, has permanently barred two men from promoting an alleged tax fraud scheme involving bogus tax credits for the production of methane gas from landfills, the Justice Department announced today.  Ronald Fontenot and Anthony Burrell consented to the civil injunction order against them without admitting wrongdoing.  The order was signed by Judge Marcia A. Crone of the U.S. District Court for the Eastern District of Texas. None None None
