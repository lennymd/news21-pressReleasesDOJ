Attorney General Eric Holder released the following statement today on President Obamas 2015 National Security Strategy:With the 2015 National Security Strategy, President Obama has laid out a credible and timely vision of strong and sustainable American leadership on a global scale. This strategy is predicated on strength, both at home and around the world, and founded on the timeless values the American people have always held sacred. In concert with our global partners  through patience, persistence, and a clear sense of purpose  this strategy will drive our efforts to confront threats, to reduce risks, and to advance our core interests of security, prosperity, and a rules-based international order.
