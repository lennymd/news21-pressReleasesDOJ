A Harvey, Illinois man pleaded guilty today to wire fraud and aggravated identity theft charges related to a scheme to obtain fraudulent tax refunds using stolen ID information, announced Acting Deputy Assistant Attorney General Stuart M. Goldberg of the Justice Departments Tax Division and U.S. Attorney Zachary T. Fardon of the Northern District of Illinois. None None None Acting Deputy Assistant Attorney General Goldberg thanked agents of the Internal Revenue Service Criminal Investigation, who conducted the investigation, and Tax Division Trial Attorneys Michael C. Boteler and Timothy M. Russo, who are prosecuting the case.
