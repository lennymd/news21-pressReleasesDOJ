  WASHINGTON  Attorney General Eric Holder today announced comprehensive steps to enhance the Justice Departments compliance with rules that require the government to turn over certain types of evidence to the defense in criminal cases.  "I am committed to ensuring that our prosecutors are provided sufficient training to understand fully their discovery obligations, and that they receive the support and resources necessary to do their jobs in a manner consistent with the proud traditions of this Department," Attorney General Holder said.  "The actions we are taking today are part of an ongoing process to ensure justice is served in every case the Department brings. We will continue to review how cases are managed before, during and after charges are filed, and where there is room for improvement, we will make additional changes," Holder said.
