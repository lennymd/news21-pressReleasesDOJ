  The Justice Department and the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) announced today that Lawrence Garafola, a resident of Poway, Calif., pleaded guilty to tax evasion.None None   Garafola faces a maximum sentence of five years in prison, three years of supervised release and a $250,000 fine. He has agreed to pay restitution of $760,637 to the IRS.   The case was investigated by IRS-Criminal Investigation. Trial Attorneys Mark Kotila and Robert Kennedy of the Justice Departments Tax Division are prosecuting this case.
