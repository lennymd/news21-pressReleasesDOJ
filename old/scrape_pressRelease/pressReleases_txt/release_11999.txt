WASHINGTON  The Justice Department today announced an agreement with the city of Port St. Lucie, Fla., to improve access to all aspects of civic life for persons with disabilities. The agreement was reached under the Departments Project Civic Access initiative to bring state and local governments into compliance with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA).
