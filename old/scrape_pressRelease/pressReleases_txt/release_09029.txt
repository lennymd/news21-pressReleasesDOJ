  WASHINGTON  Acting Assistant Attorney General Sharis Pozen of the Department of Justices Antitrust Division and Federal Trade Commission (FTC) Chairman Jon Leibowitz today met with a delegation from Chinas Ministry of Commerce (MOFCOM) to discuss antitrust merger enforcement. The delegation was led by China International Trade Representative and MOFCOM Vice Minister Gao Hucheng. MOFCOM is responsible for handling reviews of mergers and acquisitions under Chinas Antimonopoly Law. None None   Department and FTC officials said that the discussions with the delegation from MOFCOM were productive, and that they look forward to continuing their cooperative relationship.
