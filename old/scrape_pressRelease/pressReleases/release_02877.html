Readout of Attorney General Lynch's Meeting with Belgian Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Security and the Interior Jan Jambon

Attorney General Loretta Lynch and Belgian Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Security and the Interior Jan Jambon met today at the U.S. Department of Justice in Washington, D.C., and pledged continued support and cooperation between their two countries. The Attorney General and the Deputy Prime Minister discussed cooperation and collaboration in the fight against terrorism.
